Our Story

"Mother is the only person in world who can contribute their share for their children, so the story behind Surita is about a mother, a mother who has contributed her life and talent for her children. She was 15 when she realised that she wasn't made to cook food in kitchen or serve her life as normal housewives, when she started making handicrafts from the scrap and useless things. As time passed she never lost her interest and her talent grew, but in a small village she never got recoginisation for her talent. And as time passed she got married and got two beautiful children. But the perfect happy life never exists, financial problems surrounded her. Today for her children she decided to use her talent and made a platform for other women too, so that they can recognise their strength. In a present day she works with a group of women and has a small factory unit who makes handicrafts, jewellrys, etc from the raw materials made of total waste. The lady's name is 'Surita' which means "love is pain", her name is like a hope for every other women who works for their family. Our motto is to spread love which she delievers in form of her talent. There are more Suritas out there in India who are seeking for such platform, so if you know any such story about a person please share it over here and we welcome ideas from our customers about different and innovative products."
